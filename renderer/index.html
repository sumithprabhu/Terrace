How Terrace works

No accounts, no servers, no middlemen. It's just you and everyone else in the room, talking straight to each other.

What you can do

  • Predict the score before kickoff. It locks in the moment the match starts.
  • Chat live, react, reply, and @mention people in the room.
  • Watch the score update live as people call it in.
  • See the leaderboard settle once the match ends.

Under the hood

Built on Hyperswarm and Hypercore, the same tech behind Keet and other Holepunch apps. Every device keeps its own copy of the room, found and synced directly with peers. No database, no backend, nothing to shut off.

Everyone connects straight to each other. No server in the middle to go down, get hacked, or shut off.
